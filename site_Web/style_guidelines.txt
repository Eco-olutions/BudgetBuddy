Overall Look and Feel of BudgetBuddy
===============================

The overall look and feel should be minimalist with some bright colors to attract attention. 

Colours
========
For BudgetBuddy, we chose this combination of colors because it offers a harmonious blend of warmth and coolness.
These are our color swatches:

- #484A47 (Outer space)
- #5C6D70 (Payne's gray)
- #A37774 (Old rose)
- #E88873 (Salmon)
- #FFEEDB (Antique white)

Iconography
===========
We will focus on using icons that have a minimalistic design, with no added depth or shading, which adheres to the overall look and feel of BudgetBuddy.


Typography
==========
We will be using Playfair display, Abel,Inter, Besley from Google Fonts.

Links
=====
Links will not be underlined and should change color(darker shade) when a user hovers over it.

Buttons
=======
Buttons will be round edges, when you hover over each button it remains the same color but a darker shade.
